Drum Transcription App

This is a drum transcription app as my undergraduate final thesis outcome. This app uses the segment and classify method.
Hence, the audio is first segmented based on the detected onsets and the segment is classified into snare drums, hi-hats, and/or bass drums.
For the classification purposes, this app uses deep learning with [TBD] architecture trained on MDB Drums and IDMT-SMT datasets.

The details on the thesis can be found at [TBD].

How to use this app

The app is divided into three main sections:

  1. Upload - Upload an audio file to be transcribed.
  2. Transcription - The audio is segmented and classified.
  3. Download - Download the transcription results.
